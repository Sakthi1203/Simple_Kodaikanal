PINE FOREST

Pine Forest is located in the southwest of Kodaikanal. It is 4km away from kodaikanal bus stand In 1906, knowing a value of timber, Mr.Bryant started the Kodaikanala pine plantations. Forest is known for its tall pines,walking, photography,horse-riding. The most popular thing in Pine Forest is that it is the first forest close to the Solar Observatory.

History behind the forest

The pine plantation was started in 1906 by Mr.Bryant and the Pine Forest was one among the treasures of Kodai. This place will be loved by the people who are interested in photography and tourists will be more attracted due to its ambiance.
