KODAIKANAL LAKE

Kodaikanal Lake is a star-shaped manmade lake located in the Kodaikanal City. It is situated at an elevation of 2285 m.This lake resembles the Star. The lake is spread on 45 hectares and ideal for a boating expedition and for picnic.


  • Timing to visit : 6.00 AM - 5:00 PM
  • Long Enough : 1-2 hrs
  • Entry Fee : NO ENTRY FEE

History behind the lake

The lake was created by Sir Vere Henry Levinge, the then Collector of Madurai in 1863.
